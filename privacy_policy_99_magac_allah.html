Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 19, 2025

This privacy policy explains how information is handled in our mobile application, "99 Names of Allah".

Our Commitment to Privacy

The "99 Names of Allah" app does not collect, store, or share any personal information from its users. We do not ask for your name, email, location, or any other personally identifiable information. Your use of this app is completely anonymous.

Data Handled by Third-Party Services

To be fully transparent and comply with Google Play policies, we must inform you about the anonymous data handled by third-party services used to run and maintain the app.

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob

Advertising (Google AdMob)

To keep the app free, we display ads via Google AdMob. Google's ad service uses your device's unique, resettable "Advertising ID" to show relevant ads. We do not access this ID or any data linked to it. Google handles this process entirely.

More info: Google's ad policy.

Analytics & Crash Reporting (Google Play Services)

Google Play Services may collect anonymous data in case the app crashes, like device type, OS version, and time of the error. This data is never linked to your identity and is used solely to improve the app.

Security

We ensure app security. Since we do not collect personal data, your security with third-party services depends on them. We trust Google's strong security measures for protecting anonymous data.

Children’s Privacy

Our service is not directed at anyone under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy occasionally. Review this page periodically. Changes take effect immediately after posting.
